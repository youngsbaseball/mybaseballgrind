MyGrind Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 25, 2026 | Last Updated: April 25, 2026

📋 Plain-English Summary

Who we are: MyGrind is a youth baseball and softball training app operated by Young's Baseball Group, LLC.

Who uses MyGrind: Parents and legal guardians (18+) create accounts and add player profiles for their children ages 13–17. Players 18+ may create their own accounts.

What we collect: Account info (name, email, payment), and player training data (workouts, goals, stats). We never sell your data or your player's data.

Your rights: You can review, edit, or delete your information anytime by emailing coach@mygrindapp.com.

1. Introduction

MyGrind ("we," "us," "our," or the "Service") is a web-based training and development platform for youth baseball and softball players, operated by Young's Baseball Group, LLC ("Company") based in Santa Clarita, California. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, share, and protect information when you use mygrindapp.com (the "Service").

By using MyGrind, you agree to this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the Service.

2. Who Can Use MyGrind

MyGrind is designed for use by:

  • Parents and legal guardians who are at least 18 years old, who create and own accounts on behalf of their player(s).
  • Players ages 13 through 17, who use MyGrind through profiles created and owned by their parent or legal guardian.
  • Players age 18 or older, who may create their own accounts independently.

MyGrind is not intended for and does not knowingly collect information from children under 13 years of age. If we discover that we have collected information from a child under 13, we will promptly delete it.

3. Information We Collect

3.1 Information from Parents/Account Holders

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Phone number (optional)
  • Account password (stored encrypted)
  • Payment information (processed by Stripe — we never store full credit card numbers)
  • Billing address

3.2 Information About Players

Parents provide the following information about their player(s) when creating profiles:

  • First name (or nickname/initials)
  • Age
  • Sport (baseball or softball)
  • Position(s) played
  • Team name (optional)

Players generate the following information through Service use:

  • Workout logs and training entries
  • Performance statistics (entered by the player)
  • Goals and progress data
  • Streaks and achievements
  • Notes and reflections

3.3 Automatically Collected Information

  • Browser type and version
  • Device type and operating system
  • IP address (used for security and regional analytics)
  • Pages visited and features used
  • Login timestamps

4. How We Use Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Create and manage your account and player profiles
  • Provide training tracking, goal setting, and progress reports
  • Process subscription payments through Stripe
  • Send service-related emails (welcome, billing receipts, account changes)
  • Send optional marketing communications (which you can unsubscribe from anytime)
  • Improve and develop new features
  • Detect, prevent, and respond to fraud or technical issues
  • Comply with legal obligations

5. How We Share Information

We do not sell your personal information or your player's information. We share information only as follows:

5.1 Service Providers

We use trusted third-party services to operate MyGrind:

  • Stripe — for processing subscription payments. Stripe's privacy practices are governed by their Privacy Policy.
  • Mailchimp — for sending email communications. Subject to Mailchimp's Privacy Policy.
  • Hosting and infrastructure providers — to host the Service securely.

5.2 Legal Requirements

We may disclose information if required by law, valid legal process, or to protect the rights, property, or safety of MyGrind, our users, or others.

5.3 Business Transfers

If MyGrind is involved in a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred. We will notify you of any such change via email or a prominent notice on the Service.

6. Parental Rights and Controls

As the account holder, the parent or legal guardian has full control over their player's profile and data, including the right to:

  • Review all information collected about their player
  • Update or correct any information
  • Delete their player's profile or the entire account at any time
  • Refuse further data collection by closing the account
  • Export player data upon request

To exercise any of these rights, email coach@mygrindapp.com. We will respond within 30 days.

7. California Residents — Your CCPA Rights

If you are a California resident, the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) and California Privacy Rights Act (CPRA) give you the following rights:

  • Right to Know what personal information we collect, use, and share.
  • Right to Delete personal information we have collected (with some exceptions).
  • Right to Correct inaccurate personal information.
  • Right to Opt-Out of the sale or sharing of personal information. We do not sell or share personal information for cross-context behavioral advertising.
  • Right to Non-Discrimination for exercising any of these rights.
  • Right to Limit Use of sensitive personal information.

To exercise these rights, email coach@mygrindapp.com with the subject line "CCPA Request." We may need to verify your identity before fulfilling the request.

8. Data Security

We use industry-standard security measures to protect your information, including:

  • HTTPS/SSL encryption for all data in transit
  • Encrypted password storage
  • Secure payment processing through Stripe (PCI-DSS compliant)
  • Regular security reviews and updates

However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security, but we work hard to protect your information.

9. Data Retention

We retain account and player information for as long as the account is active. If you delete your account, we will delete or anonymize your information within 30 days, except where we are required to retain it for legal, tax, or fraud-prevention purposes.

10. Cookies and Tracking

MyGrind uses essential cookies to keep you logged in and remember your preferences. We do not use third-party advertising cookies or behavioral tracking. You can control cookies through your browser settings, but disabling them may limit Service functionality.

11. Children's Privacy (Under 13)

MyGrind is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us at coach@mygrindapp.com and we will take steps to delete that information.

12. International Users

MyGrind is operated from the United States. If you access the Service from outside the U.S., your information will be transferred to and processed in the United States, where data protection laws may differ from those in your country.

13. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will notify you by email and post a prominent notice on the Service. The "Last Updated" date at the top reflects when changes were made. Continued use of the Service after changes means you accept the updated Privacy Policy.
